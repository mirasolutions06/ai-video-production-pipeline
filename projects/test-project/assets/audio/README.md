# Audio Assets

## Background Music (optional)

Place a royalty-free music track here as `music.mp3`.

**Recommended sources:**
- Epidemic Sound (epidemicsound.com) — paid, broadcast-quality
- Pixabay Music (pixabay.com/music) — free
- Freesound (freesound.org) — free with attribution
- Artlist (artlist.io) — paid, unlimited license

**Requirements:**
- Format: MP3 (preferred) or WAV
- Sample rate: 44100 Hz
- Bitrate: 192 kbps or higher
- Duration: Longer than the final video (pipeline loops/trims automatically)

## Sound Effects (optional)

Place .mp3 or .wav files inside the `sfx/` folder.
Name them descriptively, e.g. `swoosh.mp3`, `ding.wav`.

## Voiceover

Voiceover is AUTO-GENERATED by ElevenLabs from the script in config.json.
The output is saved to: `../../output/audio/voiceover.mp3`
